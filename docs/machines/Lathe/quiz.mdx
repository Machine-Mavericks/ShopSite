<DragDropQuiz name="CNC Router">
<ol>
    <li>Wear <Blank answer="safety glasses" /> whenever around a CNC machine that is in operation.</li>
    <li>Stand <Blank answer="back" /> from the CNC while it is operating.</li>
    <li>Never try to <Blank answer="adjust" /> the material while the machine is running.</li>
    <li>Secure your work securely to the CNC table with <Blank answer="jigs" /> or <Blank answer="clamps" />.</li>
    <li>Ensure that any clamps or jigs will not interfere with the <Blank answer="movement" /> of the gantry or router.</li>
    <li>Never leave the machine running <Blank answer="unattended" />.</li>
    <li>After running your program, be sure to <Blank answer="clean" /> the CNC table and <Blank answer="trackways" /> of all debris.</li>
    <li>Whenever possible do a <Blank answer="test" /> run of your program with the router turned <Blank answer="off" />, and before putting your material on the table.</li>
    <li>Be aware of <Blank answer="pinch points" /> on the CNC router. Keep fingers and material clear of these points.</li>
    <li>Keep the CNC table <Blank answer="clear" /> of material not being machined, and that nothing has been placed near the CNC that will be hit by the gantry.</li>
</ol>
